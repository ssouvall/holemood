How it Works

One batch = 8 bagels.
Cost per batch = $20

Orders are made on a first-come, first-served basis and can be placed using the contact form below or by phone.

Bagels are made Monday-Friday and will be ready for pickup or delivery after 11am unless otherwise specified.

Payment can be made via PayPal, Venmo, or cash.

We are a small shop and request your patience as demand is very high!
